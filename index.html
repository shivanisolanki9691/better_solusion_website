Better your solution in Business


Define the problem

Ask yourself what the problem is. A clear understanding of the problem is the first step in solving it. If you are discussing a problem with a team
member, don't assume that all parties involved define the problem in the same manner.

List possible solutions to the problem

Go to a white board or flip chart and write out a list of possible solutions. It pays to spend extra time on the list, especially if the problem is
rather complex in nature or requires communication with a team member, department or remote division to solve the problem.
